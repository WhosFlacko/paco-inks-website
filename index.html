Aftercare

Protect Your Investment

01

First 24 Hours

Leave the bandage on for 2-4 hours. Gently wash with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap. Pat dry — never rub. Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturizer or aftercare balm.

02

Days 2-14

Wash 2-3 times daily with clean hands. Apply a thin layer of aftercare product. Don't scratch, pick, or peel flaking skin. Avoid tight clothing over the tattoo.

03

Weeks 2-4

Continue moisturizing daily. Avoid direct sunlight, pools, hot tubs, and saunas. The tattoo may look cloudy — this is normal and will clear as it heals.

04

Long-Term Care

Always apply SPF 30+ sunscreen over your tattoo when exposed to sun. Keep the skin moisturized. Touch-ups are available if needed — just reach out.
